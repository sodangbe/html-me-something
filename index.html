MY QUEST TO 100 HALF MARATHONS

The beginning

In 2015 , after a routine visit at the doctor , I discovered that I needed to make healthy choices . I was obese at 250 lbs .My healthy choices included more water , and up to 10 hours swimming , biking and running. Just like that I fell in love training for triathlons and marathons

The journey

In november 2015 , 6 months after my decision to make healthy choices , I started a training program for the bentonville half marathon. Running is way harder than swimming and biking . Increasing the run mileage every week grew my confidence and my energy. I built during that first training a discipline for daily runs.I was not going to let that discipline wasted,that is why as soon as I finished the bentonville half martahon in april 2015 , my next decision was to run 100 half marathons in 5 years.My quest took me around the country to run 46 scenics and challenging races (My races) . In 2016 , finished the year with 16 half marathons and 1 marathon.In 2017, I raced in 17 half marathons and 1 full marathon .My goal was on track until .....

The fall

Yes, you may have guessed it on my last race of 2017 , I tored a ligament in my left knee. I was devasted because I was not even able to swim. I had no choice but to forfeit 2018 ,in order to recover from the intense running from the past two years.2019 Will be my year to continue my quest . Well you may have guessed it , another injury to my right knee sidelined me immediately .I was disappointed but I do not quit , i will rest both knee and be back in force in 2020....We all kow now that 2020 did not happened as planned as races after races were being cancel everywhere and gyms closed ..2021 here I come ...

Never quit>

I do excel at restarting over and over .I failed tree times to graduate for high school but I was able to restart everytime because I never lost the goal to attend college . Perseverence is a trait that I admire about the running community and I will never quit until I am back running to my 100th half marathons. Until then take a look a some of my favorites races
